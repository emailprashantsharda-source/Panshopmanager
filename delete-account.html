Delete My Account

This page explains how to permanently delete your hisaabnow account and all associated data.

⚠️ This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your sales records, customer data, inventory, and shop information will be deleted forever. Please export any data you want to keep before deleting.

What gets deleted

  • Your phone number and login credentials
  • Your shop name and profile information
  • All sales records and transaction history
  • Customer ledger and udhar (credit) records
  • Inventory and product list
  • Expense records
  • WhatsApp share history
  • Voice billing data
  • AI conversation history

What we may keep (for legal reasons)

  • Payment records: Kept for 7 years as required by Indian tax law (GST compliance). These records are anonymized — they show only the transaction amount and date, not your shop activity.
  • Crash reports: Anonymous error logs may remain in our system for up to 90 days to help us fix bugs.

How to delete your account

✅ Easiest way (instant):
  1. Open the hisaabnow app
  2. Go to More → Settings
  3. Scroll down to Danger Zone
  4. Tap "Delete My Account"
  5. Type DELETE to confirm
  6. Your account will be deleted immediately
Don't have access to the app anymore? If you've uninstalled the app or lost your phone, fill out the form below and we will delete your account within 7 days.

Verification

For security, we may ask you to verify your identity by sending an OTP to your registered phone number before deleting the account. This prevents unauthorized deletion requests.

What happens after deletion

  1. Your account is marked for deletion immediately
  2. You can no longer log in
  3. All personal data is permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days
  4. Backups are purged within 90 days
  5. You are free to create a new account anytime in the future using the same phone number
Active subscription? If you have an active hisaabnowpro subscription, deleting your account will not automatically refund the unused portion. Please contact us before deletion if you'd like to request a refund.
